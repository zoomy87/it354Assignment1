What tech I like

I am a huge fan of Raspberry Pis and have three at home one of which I am using as a webserver and is currently hosting a website for a class project. I have plans to build a magic mirror that is on hold till I have some free time. If you don't know a magic mirror is a TV with a two-way mirror over the screen and a Raspberry Pi connected to it. This gives the appearance that the "mirror" has words or images magically appearing. This can display weather, traffic, email, schedule, and a myriad other things that one could want. Another tech that I am a fan of is Spring and I am constantly impressed with how much it can do and how quickly an enterprise level API can be set up.
